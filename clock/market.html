Versor × Market

Do the versor / lunar / seasonal cycles line up with anything in the markets? Usually the honest answer is “barely.” That’s the point — see for yourself.

⚠ Research / curiosity tool — not financial advice. Past patterns don’t predict future returns. We test many buckets at once, so some “signal” shows up by pure chance (p-values are uncorrected for multiple testing). Don’t trade on this.

Method: for each trading day we compute the versor/lunar/solar/weekly phase, take the forward return over the chosen horizon, and average it within each phase bucket. “Spread” = best-minus-worst bucket mean; the p-value is a 300-run permutation test (how often random labels produce a spread that large). Longer horizons overlap, which inflates apparent significance — next-day is the cleanest. *Even a “beyond chance” result here is uncorrected for multiple testing and is not tradeable. ← back to the clock
