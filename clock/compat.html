About this reading & how it’s computed

Each chart is computed in your browser from the vendored astronomy-engine (offline, no API), exactly as on the Natal Blueprint page. The compatibility reading overlays the two charts and classifies every shared channel as one of the four classic connection types — electromagnetic (each holds one end), companionship (both hold the whole channel), dominance (one holds the whole channel, the other neither end), or compromise (one holds the whole channel, the other one end) — then reads centres, types and authorities for the pair. Lineage: informed by Human Design & Gene Keys; all interpretive language is our own — a symbolic, hypothesis-generating mirror, not deterministic fact, and not medical, psychological, or relationship advice.
