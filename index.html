The mechanism

The Subconscious Inception Method

S.I.M. is a three-move protocol for swapping one subconscious program for a better one. Same mechanism therapists use to dissolve PTSD (EMDR), trauma (IFS), and panic (EFT). Not a mantra you say. A program you install.

  1. 01

    See

    Find the program running in the background. You can't change an app that isn't open.

  2. 02

    Choose

    Pick a better one from a clearer playbook. Not a wish. An upgrade.

  3. 03

    Install

    Hold the new program while the old one is still running. Your brain swaps the wiring.

The promise: three scales

The work compounds outward. What you change in yourself changes the room. What you build in yourself becomes the floor everyone else stands on.

  • For yourself

    You, in the driver's seat. The programs that used to run you start losing their grip. The version of you that wins more days than it loses becomes the default.

  • For your loved ones

    The room you walk into changes. Your kids, your partner, your team. Their nervous systems sync with yours whether you mean to or not. The work you do on yourself shows up in them.

  • For your lineage

    The wake you leave. Grandkids who'll never meet you. Mentees who never knew you. Whatever pattern you broke in yourself is the pattern they don't have to break.

The version of you that walks into a room shapes the version of everyone who walks out.
